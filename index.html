About Cedar Dough

A family-run bakery with real warmth behind it.

Jalila and Peter start before dawn so the dough is ready for the first customer through the door. Everything is made with care, using flavours that feel true to Lebanese tradition and easy to love from the first bite.

Customers know Cedar Dough for fresh food, genuine hospitality and the kind of welcome that turns a quick stop into a regular habit.

Why it matters

A bakery with a real heartbeat behind it.

This is not a chain and it does not try to act like one. Cedar Dough feels personal because it is personal. People come for the food, then come back for the warmth.

Made before sunrise

Jalila starts the day before most of Chatswood is awake, preparing dough from scratch so the first manoush out of the oven tastes as fresh as it should.

Lebanese at its heart

Zaatar, lahem be ajine, kafta, handmade pies and wraps form the core of the menu, with something approachable for first-timers and deeply familiar for those who grew up with these flavours.

The kind of place people talk about

Review after review describes the same thing: generous food, genuine hospitality and an atmosphere closer to visiting a friend than grabbing a quick bite.
